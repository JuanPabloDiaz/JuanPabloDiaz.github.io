Rah's Best Defense

Rah's self defense program is a workout for women who have felt physically threatened by others. In my class I will teach you the fundamentals of self-defense and AS; which is an acronym for Active Sight. Students who have graduated from this class have a greater sense of self confidence and awareness. They have also come back to me for recommendations to further their knowledge of Self-Defense.

I started this program in order to heighten self-awareness and confidence of women all over the state. I feel that women think that if they are attacked by a man, it will be a losing fight. All women should know this is not true, no matter your degree of strength, you can defend and win a fight with a man. The techniques I teach in this course are NOT all defensive. I feel you also need to know offensive movements to show your opponent you know what your doing.
